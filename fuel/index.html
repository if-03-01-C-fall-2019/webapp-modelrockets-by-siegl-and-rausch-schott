Manual for Rocketfuel

DISCLAIMER: We do NOT recommend you to buy, own or use KNO3. KNO3 can be used to build explosives, therefore there are strong restrictions.


  1.  1   Get everything you need

    The first step is of course, to get every item we need. For a rocket fuel you will need the following things:

    • Blender
    • 60gram KNO3 (Blended)
    • 40gram sugar (Blended)
    • Some cat litter (Blended)
    • Frying Pan
    • Electric heater (do not use a gasburner it could cause an fire in combination with KNO3 and sugar)
    • Electric Scale
    • Containment for your Fuel

  2.  2   Cooking the actual fuel

    For the next step you have to mix 60grams KNO3 powder with 40grams sugar powder to get a 60:40 rotio between the sugar and the KNO3 Heat the powder mixture in the frying pan. In the beginning it is important to mix the powder in the pan so the heat can equally distribute within the podwer. After some minutes you should see the sugar caramelizing. Mix the powder as long as you see sugar that is caramelized.


  3.  3   Waiting for the right moment

    When the mixture looks like above its time to get a containment and fill the mixture into it.


  4.  4   Let the fuel dry

    When you filled the fuel into the containment for it its time to let it dry until it looks like the image above.


  5.  5   Time to try it out!

    When every thing dried and if you're ready to go it's time to test your selfmade rocket engine! Be aware that this is very dangerous so we advice you to ignite the engine electricly so that you can have enough seafty distance to the engine.
